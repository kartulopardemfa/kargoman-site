Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KargoMan handles information when you use the game on iPhone and iPad devices.

1. What KargoMan stores directly

KargoMan stores gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as progress, settings, and unlocked states. This data is used to run the game experience and is not tied to an account system operated by us.

2. Advertising

KargoMan uses Google AdMob to serve ads. Ad delivery and ad measurement may involve the collection or processing of technical information by Google and its partners, including device identifiers, diagnostics, ad interaction events, and approximate location information where applicable.

Ads may be served as personalized or non-personalized depending on the user’s region, consent state, tracking permission status, and ad privacy choices. Google’s consent tooling may present consent and tracking prompts where required before ad requests begin.

3. Analytics and accounts

KargoMan does not currently require account creation and does not operate a dedicated first-party backend for profiles, cloud saves, or social features.

4. Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party services and SDKs, including Google AdMob. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Help Center

5. Your choices

You can stop further local data storage by deleting the app from your device. You can also manage certain advertising and privacy settings through your device and Apple account settings. KargoMan also provides an in-app ad privacy choices entry point inside Settings so you can revisit supported ad consent options.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by changing the effective date on this page.

7. Contact

For support or privacy questions, please use the support page linked below.
